# Code of Conduct

Open Intelligence Lab is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and ethical research environment.

## Standards
Participants are expected to:
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Show respect and empathy  
- Provide constructive feedback  
- Focus on community benefit  

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Publishing private information  
- Introducing unethical or harmful data  

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may:
- Issue warnings  
- Remove contributions  
- Ban repeat offenders  

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a confidential issue or contact the maintainers directly.
